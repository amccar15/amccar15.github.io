Website Evaluation

Good Website!

Why is this a good design?

  • C- Contrast: This website has very good contrast with a light back ground and unique element design. The size and shape of the elements are all different, bringing attention to them in order of importance.
  • R- Repetition: This website utilize repetition well, each section is similar to its respective group. Therefore the user never feels lost at any point.
  • A- Alignment: This website has mastered alignment, each section along with there connecting sections are all in line with the margins and each other. This gives good direction for the user to follow the intended flow of the webpage.
  • P- Proximity: Elements that are similar are together, stories, films, anything on the page is set at a proper proximity to other elements. As a user it easy to understand what goes where.

Bad Website!

Why is this a bad design?

  • C- Contrast: This website has contrast but not in the correct way. A lot of the text is hard to read or even determine what it is your looking at. Most of the text is legiable, however the background is incredibly distracting.
  • R- Repetition: There is a good bit of repetition on this webpage. However, The left margin is grouped with all the types of vehicles, towards the bottom of the page there is a ton of white space, and it loses it's repetition.
  • A- Alignment: The center of this website has decent alignment, but that is roughly where it stops. There are tons of boxs and sections that are out of line or placed randomly on the page.
  • P- Proximity: Grouping was done partially well, the left margin is grouped and the center has a similar group so a user could understand what they were looking at. However, elements that are grouped together correctly are grouped right by other groups and it becomes confusing.
